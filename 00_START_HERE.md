## 🚀 Quick Start (30 seconds)

1. **Open the App**
   - Double-click: `index.html`
   - It opens in your browser - ready to use!

2. **Create a Project** (Optional)
   - Click "Projects" in sidebar
   - Enter project name and hourly rate
   - Click "Create Project"

3. **Start Tracking**
   - Click "Time Tracker"
   - Select project
   - Click "Start"
   - Work for a bit
   - Click "Stop"
   - ✅ Earnings calculated!

4. **View Dashboard**
   - Click "Dashboard"
   - See earnings, expenses, profit
   - View charts and activity

## 💰 How Earnings Work

Formula: **Hours × Rate = Earnings**

Example:
- You worked 2 hours
- Your rate: $50/hour
- Earnings: $100 ✅

Multi-project example:
- Project A: 2 hours × $50 = $100
- Project B: 3 hours × $60 = $180
- Total: $280

---

## 💸 How Profit Works

Formula: **Earnings - Expenses = Profit**

Example:
- Earnings: $500
- Expenses: $100 (software, internet)
- Profit: $400 ✅

Profit Margin: $400 / $500 = **80%**
